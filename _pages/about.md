Hi! My name is Zixiao Zhao. I'm currently a PhD student in School of Computer Science in the [University of Auckland](https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en.html), under the supervision of Associate Professor [Jing Sun](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jing-sun). My research topic focuses on Auto-Software Engineering for Medical Image Processing.

## Education Experience
I got my Bacholar of Engineering Degree in **Xidian University** (Xi'an University of Electronic Science and Technology, China), majored in Microelectronics. I then pursued my study in France and got my Engineer's Degree (equals to Master Degree) of Imaging and Electronics in **Telecom Saint-Etienne**. At the meantime, I also achieved a dual Master's Degree of Advanced Imaging and Material Appearance in **University Jean Monnet**. Now, I'm studying in **University of Auckland**, New Zealand for my PhD.
